The Human Mosaic invites people from around the world to become part of a monumental artwork. Each participant will choose a room, claim a position, and contribute to an immersive installation designed to become a permanent museum experience in Italy.

The Three Rooms

Three themes. Three immersive environments. Three million participants.

Identity

Who we are and where we come from.

Selfies that reflect who we are and where we come from, including monuments, traditions, cultural symbols, local roots, and personal identity.

Love

What we love defines us.

Family, partners, friends, pets, passions, and meaningful moments that represent love in all its forms.

Creativity

Human imagination without limits.

Art, photography, drawings, concepts, and creative expression. Artistic nudity allowed. Explicit sexual content prohibited.
