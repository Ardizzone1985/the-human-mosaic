A collective artwork built one face at a time.

The Human Mosaic invites people from around the world to become part of a monumental artwork. Each participant will choose a room, claim a position, and contribute to an immersive installation designed to become a permanent museum experience in Italy.

The Three Rooms

Three themes. Three immersive environments. Three million participants.

Identity

Who we are and where we come from.

Selfies that reflect who we are and where we come from, including monuments, traditions, cultural symbols, local roots, and personal identity.

Love

What we love defines us.

Family, partners, friends, pets, passions, and meaningful moments that represent love in all its forms.

Creativity

Human imagination without limits.

Art, photography, drawings, concepts, and creative expression. Artistic nudity allowed. Explicit sexual content prohibited.

HOW IT WORKS

From reservation to official participation.

The process is simple, structured, and designed to guide each participant from room selection to final confirmation inside the project.

01

Choose your room

Select the thematic space that best represents your contribution: Identity, Love, or Creativity.

02

Select your wall and spot

Reserve a real position inside the mosaic by choosing your wall, section, and individual spot.

03

Complete payment

Confirm your participation through the official checkout process and secure your reserved position.

04

Upload your image

Submit the image that will officially represent your place inside The Human Mosaic.

05

Wait for review

Each submission is reviewed within 48 hours to ensure it aligns with the project guidelines and selected room.

06

Receive your certificate

Approved participants receive an official digital certificate confirming their place inside the project.
